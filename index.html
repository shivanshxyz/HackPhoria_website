F.A.Q

  • What is the eligibility criteria?

    A team should consist of undergraduates and/or school students from grades 11th/12th. A Team can have atmost 4 members and minimum 2

  • How much is the cost for Registration?

    Hola! There is no registration charges.We will also be providing meals ,drinks, snacks , courtsey of our sponsors.

  • What should I bring to the event?

    You need to carry your college/school ID Card, laptop, phone, chargers, any sleeping equipment you might want and a geeky t-shirt!

  • Can I start working on my hack before the event?

    No. Since it wouldn't be fair to other contestents, every team is supposed to start on their projects on the day of the hackathon!

  • Do I need to keep working throughout the event ?

    Everybody is free to work for as long as they want. But to make a good project it is advised to make full use of the time. Scheduled breaks are there for meals.

  • Are there any prizes for the girls team?

    It is rightly said, "It is time that we all see gender as a spectrum instead of two sets of opposing ideals. "prizes will be awarded to the best projects irrespective of gender and social category.

  • Is there any travel reimbursement?

    There is no travel reimbursement at present. Participants will be informed if there will be any change in plan.

  • What if i have never been to a hackathon before.?

    "You dont have to be great to start, but you have to start to be great." Hackphoria will be an amazing opportunity to get started. Although having some technical skill might help , it is not a necessity. We will be having mentors and speakers throughout the event to help the participants

  • Dont find my question .what should I do?

    Don't worry ! Reach us at hackphoria@gmail.com or ieee@bennett.edu.in
