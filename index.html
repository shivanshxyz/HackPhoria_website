Technotix is a student-led club that works towards developing a community of excited learners in the field of Robotics-related technology. The Club provides a place where all students, especially those with an inclination for technology, can thrive. The club caters to the interests of hobbyists and beginners in robotics and extends support for participation in robotics-related competitions that are held all over the country. The club's intent is to inspire young minds to be the driving force in the field of robotics, by engaging them in exciting, mentor-based programs that build engineering skills, inspire innovation, and foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication and leadership.
